Digital Electronics

worksheet #1, worksheet #2, worksheet #3 (will be distributed in class)

Digital electronics is the interface between computer science and electrical engineering. In fact, it can be considered the core of computer engineering, which is a college major offered at many universities. Digital electronics is very closely related to the study of Boolean Algebra an duses most of the same operators. However digital electronics focuses on schematic diagrams that indicate the physical design of circuits.

Study the symbols and operators and apply the Boolean logic operations correctly.
